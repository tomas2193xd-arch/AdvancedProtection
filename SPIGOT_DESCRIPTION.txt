[SIZE=5][B][COLOR=#FFD700]üìã Commands[/COLOR][/B][/SIZE]

[B]Player Commands:[/B]
[CODE]/ap menu - Open the main protection menu
/ap visualize - Show protection borders
/ap trust <player> - Add a member to your protection
/ap untrust <player> - Remove a member
/ap trustlist - View all members
/ap rename <name> - Rename your protection
/ap clearname - Remove custom name
/ap paytax - Pay protection tax
/ap help - Show command list[/CODE]

[B]Admin Commands:[/B]
[CODE]/ap reload - Reload configuration
/ap logs - View protection logs
/ap manager - Open admin management panel[/CODE]
